Watches

Lastly, a very unhealthy have hobby of mines is watch collecting. Currently, I own eight watches. My most expensive watch would be my Cartier tank. It was a watch that I saved up quite a while for and cost me a little over $2000. My grail watch would be either the Cartier Crash or the Cheich. The only downside of owning watches from Cartier is that they don't retain value. Unlike the two watches I mentioned earlier, unless you own a Patek Philippe or Rolex, your watch really isn't retaining value. Nevertheless, I do favor Cartier over all other watch brands.
