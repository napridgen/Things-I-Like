Brunch

I love food, I’m sure someone else is going to say that, because most people love food. However, I enjoy brunch the most. Brunch is not only about eating breakfast at any time of the day, it’s also about socializing with the people who are most important to you.

Fall

Fall is my favorite time of year. Mainly because Fall involves all of my favorite colors: Olive green, burnt orange and, mustard yellow. Also, I do love the fall drink selections when it comes to Starbucks. Not to mention the fall selection when it comes to Bath and Bodyworks.

Fragrances

Speaking of bath and Bodyworks, I am a fragrance connoisseur. I collect different perfumes and candles. My favorite candle is teakwood mahogany intense from Bath and Bodyworks and my favorite fall fragrance to wear is a Tom Ford black orchid. It’s considered to be a woman’s fragrance, but most people would say it’s unisex.
